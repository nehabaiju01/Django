ABOUT THE PROJECT


A beautifully designed flower store that allow users to effortlessly explore, purchase, and manage a wide variety of flowers sourced directly from gardens. With its intuitive interface and powerful CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionality, revolutionizes the flower buying experience, bringing convenience and joy to flower enthusiasts and gift seekers alike.The user can login as a customer and manager.Customer can purchase products whereas manager can add the products to be sold.Customers can purchase from the nurseries nearby in the "Nursery Near Me" section and see the products purchased in "My Orders".They have an option to cancel the items purchased. Users can give their suggestions to the admin by clicking on "Contact Us". The Manager can view the order details in the "Manage Orders" section and add new product details in "Mangage Products" section.
